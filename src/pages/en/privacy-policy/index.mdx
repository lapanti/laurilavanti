## Lauri Lavanti's electronic newsletter

Lauri Lavanti publishes an electronic newsletter, which you can subscribe to through Lauri Lavanti's website. The newsletter is sent by email to subscribers (opt-in). Personal data is used solely for sending the newsletter.

Only the email address of subscribers is stored. Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for sending the newsletter (until the subscription is canceled or the newsletter service is discontinued).

Each newsletter includes an option for subscribers to unsubscribe. The personal data of those who unsubscribe is promptly deleted.

### Disclosure and transfer of personal data

MailerLite Limited acts as the data processor on behalf of Lauri Lavanti.

Personal data is not disclosed for direct marketing, opinion research, or market research purposes.

Personal data is processed within the EU/EEA area. Personal data will not be transferred to third countries or international organizations.

### Your data protection rights regarding the processing of personal data

**Right to information about the processing of personal data**
- You have the right to know the purposes and methods of processing your personal data.
- We aim to provide a comprehensive overview of our data protection practices and the processing of personal data in our operations through this description. If any aspect of our processing remains unclear, we have not succeeded in our goal. In such cases, you may ask for clarification by sending your questions to Lauri Lavanti via email (see contact details below).
- More information about the right to receive information about the processing of personal data.

**Right of access**
- You have the right to know whether we process your personal data. If we do, you have the right to obtain a copy of this data unless we have a legal basis to refuse the request.

**Right to rectification**
- If the personal data we process about you is inaccurate, you may request that we correct it.

**Right to restrict processing**
- If you believe the personal data we process about you is inaccurate, processed unlawfully, or you have objected to the processing, you may request that we restrict the processing of your data.

You can submit requests regarding your data protection rights to Lauri Lavanti via email (lauri@laurilavanti.fi).
