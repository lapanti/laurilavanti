Finland has spent decades building technological expertise — from Vaisala and icebreakers to Nokia, Linux and Supercell. That foundation is now under pressure, and at the same time AI is transforming work, digital services and human connection faster than legislators can respond.

Too often, decision-making lacks an understanding of how technology works and how it affects society. I hold a Master of Science in Engineering from Aalto University, and have over a decade of experience building secure software and banking services. I know how these systems work — and what happens when they don't.
