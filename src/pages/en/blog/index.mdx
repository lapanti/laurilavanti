In this blog, I explore how technology, especially artificial intelligence, is transforming our society—work, education, democracy, and even humanity’s place in the world. In my writings, I address topics such as why AI does not merely enhance productivity but forces us to rethink our entire social security system, why digital sovereignty is essential for a secure and freedom-preserving society, and how we can ensure that the benefits of technological progress are accessible to all, not just a privileged few.

## What does it mean to live in the age of AI?

I believe that technology should serve humanity, not the other way around. Therefore, in my writings, I emphasize sustainable, human-centered solutions—whether the focus is on the transformation of work, the reform of education, or the safeguarding of democratic dialogue. My goal is to provoke thought, offer new perspectives, and inspire conversation about the kind of future we want to build together.
